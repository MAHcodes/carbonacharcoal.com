Product Features

  • 100% natural coconut shell.
  • No smell and taste.
  • No sparks or smoke.
  • No wood or chemicals.
  • Produce very little ash.
  • Longer, hotter, and cleaner burn.
  • Completely sulfur-free.

Lighting Instructions

  • Ignite the charcoal and wait until they're fully covered with a thin layer of ash.
  • Light the charcoal using a burner or stove.
  • Do not use the coals until they are completely burned through.

Safety Instructions

  • Store in a cool, dry place, out of reach of children.
  • Never use petroleum-based products to light the charcoal.
  • Avoid lighting charcoal in confined spaces without proper ventilation.
  • Always extinguish burning charcoal after use for your safety.
